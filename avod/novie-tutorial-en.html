    • Download files in AVOD network

    Video file:
    1. Wait for the page to load (after the progress bar at the bottom disappears)
    2. Click on the video to play
    3. Click on the video background
    4. Select the operation after the pop-up tab
    Other files:

    When you click other file links, it will automatically jump to the download page. You can copy https://www.browserdocumentsapp.com/demo/ to the web page for access experience.

    Can’t pop up tabs when clicking on the video background?

    Please use the following method to try again
    1. Refresh the current page (click the more button at the bottom right, click refresh and try again)
    2. After saving to the bookmark, open the link from the bookmark page and try again

    Transfer files from computer

    1. Click on the computer in the connection tab

    2. Copy the link and open it in the computer browser

    3. Upload files in the computer browser

    4. Note: Please ensure that the phone and computer are connected to the same WIFI

    Baidu Netdisk

    1. Log in to your Baidu account

    2. Find the file you want to download

    3. Click the download button on the right to start downloading

    Player gesture operation

    Operation Gesture
    Hide/Show the control interface Tap the player
    Fast forward and fast rewind Swipe left and right
    Change the brightness Swipe up and down on the left
    Change the volume Swipe up and down on the right
    Take screenshots/cut video clips/cut GIFs Click the scissors icon on the right
    Double speed Tap the more operation icon in the upper right corner
    Fast forward 2 times speed Long press the player

    How to return from the web page to the desktop

    Click the more button on the bottom right, and click “Return to Desktop”

    Other questions or suggestions

    If you have any other questions or suggestions, please send an email to avod.suggest@gmail.com
